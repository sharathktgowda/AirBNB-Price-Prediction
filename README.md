# AirBNB-Price-Prediction

Getting a cool place to lodge at an affordable price when travel outside home is one thing that is considered before embarking on such journey. Most
people spend time on internet to search and schedule their accommodation. A site like Airbnb is the best platform for booking spaces also gives opportunity 
to share spaces with people. An appealing affordable price is one of the key points for both the host and occupant. Evaluating a property on
Airbnb may be a difficult undertaking for the owner as a result of it decides the amount of clients for the spot. Similarly, traveller or the 
occupants got to assess the offered cost with critical information of value estimated on the property. In this paper, a forecast model is build to predict the
price using the random forest and multiple linear regression algorithms which will help both customers and the host to reach an agreement.
